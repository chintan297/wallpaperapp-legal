# Wallpaper App – Privacy Policy

This repository contains the official Privacy Policy for the **Wallpaper App**.

## Purpose

The Privacy Policy is hosted publicly to comply with **Google Play Store** requirements and to clearly communicate how user data is handled.

## App Summary

The Wallpaper App:

- Displays wallpaper images
- Allows users to browse wallpapers
- Supports marking wallpapers as favorites
- Uses a clean, single-screen design
- Does not require login or registration

## Data Collection

The app:

- ❌ Does not collect personal data
- ❌ Does not use analytics
- ❌ Does not display ads
- ❌ Does not track users

Favorites and preferences are stored **locally on the user’s device only**.

## Hosting

- This Privacy Policy is hosted via **GitHub Pages**
- The URL is used in the **Google Play Console**
- No custom domain is required

## Updating the Policy

If the app’s features change (for example, ads or analytics are added in the future), this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

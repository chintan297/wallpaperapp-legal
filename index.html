Privacy Policy

Read our privacy policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Wallpaper App ("the app") handles user information. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

The app allows users to browse and view wallpaper images in a grid and slider format. Users may mark wallpapers as favorites for quick access.

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user information.

The app does not require user registration, login, or authentication.

Favorite wallpapers and user preferences are stored locally on the user’s device only. This data never leaves the device and is not transmitted to any server.

The app accesses the internet solely to load wallpaper images from publicly available sources. No personal data is transmitted during this process.

The app does not use analytics services, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or third-party tools that collect user data.

The app does not request any sensitive device permissions.

The app is suitable for a general audience and does not knowingly collect information from children.

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future if the app’s functionality changes. Any updates will be published on this page.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the Google Play Store listing.

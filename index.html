Privacy Policy for ZenWall

This Privacy Policy explains how ZenWall handles user information. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

ZenWall allows users to browse and view high-quality dark-themed wallpapers in a grid and slider format. Users may mark wallpapers as favorites for quick access and save them locally to their device.

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user information.

The app does not require user registration, login, or authentication.

Local Data Storage: Favorite wallpapers and user preferences are stored locally on the user’s device only. This data never leaves the device and is not transmitted to any server.

Internet Access: The app accesses the internet solely to load wallpaper images from publicly available sources (such as Picsum Photos). No personal data is transmitted during this process.

Permissions: ZenWall requests the following permissions for specific functionality:

  • Storage (Photos/Media): Required only if you choose to download and save a wallpaper to your device's gallery. This permission is not accessed for any other purpose.
  • Internet: Required to fetch and display wallpapers.

The app does not use analytics services, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or third-party tools that collect user data.

The app is suitable for a general audience and does not knowingly collect information from children.

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future if the app’s functionality changes. Any updates will be published on this page.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the Google Play Store listing support email.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the Wallpaper App ("the app") handles user information. Your privacy is important to us.

Information Collection

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

User Accounts

The app does not require user registration, login, or authentication.

Data Storage

Any user preferences such as favorite wallpapers are stored locally on the device only. This data never leaves your device.

Internet Usage

The app accesses the internet solely to load wallpaper images from publicly available sources. No personal data is transmitted during this process.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use analytics tools, advertising services, or third-party SDKs that collect user data.

Permissions

The app does not request sensitive device permissions.

Children’s Privacy

The app is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the app listing on Google Play.

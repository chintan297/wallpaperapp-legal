Privacy Policy for ZenWall

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

ZenWall is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information
We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information (such as your name, email address, or phone number).

Usage Data
We do not track your usage or collect analytics data.

2. Local Data Storage

The App uses local storage on your device to provide specific features:

  • Favorites: Your list of favorite wallpapers is stored locally on your device. This data is strictly local and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties. If you clear the app data or uninstall the App, this list will be lost.

3. Third-Party Services and Content

Unsplash
The App fetches wallpaper images from Unsplash (https://unsplash.com) via their public API.

  • When you view or download a wallpaper, your device communicates directly with Unsplash's servers.
  • Unsplash may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review Unsplash's Privacy Policy for more information.

4. Device Permissions

To function correctly, the App requires the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required to fetch wallpaper images from Unsplash.
  • Photos / Media Library: Required only if you choose to download a wallpaper to your device. We use this permission solely to save the image file to your gallery. We do not access or read your existing photos.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We recommend reviewing this page periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: zenwallapp@gmail.com
